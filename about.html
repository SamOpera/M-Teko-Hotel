About Us
At M-TEKO Hotels & Service Apartments, we believe comfort shouldn’t come at a premium. Nestled in a quiet corner of Ibadan, our hotel offers guests a peaceful retreat with all the essentials — and more. From spacious rooms to reliable service, every stay is designed to be simple, seamless, and satisfying. Whether you're a traveler, a professional on assignment, or just passing through, we offer the right space for rest and recharge.

Our History
What started as a modest lodging facility has grown into a respected name in Ibadan’s hospitallity scene. M-TEKO was founded to fill a gap — a place that balances affordability with genuine hospitallity. Built on the values of consistency, cleanliness, and care, our journey has been one of steady growth driven by guest loyalty and word-of-mouth.

Mission
To deliver dependable lodging experiences by combining functionality, cleanliness, and friendliness — ensuring every guest leaves rested and satisfied.

Vision
To be recognized as a go-to hospitallity brand in Nigeria — known for simplicity, trust, and a commitment to everyday excellence.
